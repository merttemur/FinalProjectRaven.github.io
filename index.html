The Challenge

GPS Dependency Creates Critical Vulnerabilities

Modern unmanned aerial vehicles face significant operational risks when GPS signals are unavailable, jammed, or spoofed

GPS Jamming

Hostile interference can block GPS signals, leaving drones unable to determine their position or navigate safely

Critical Risk

GPS Spoofing

Malicious actors can send false GPS signals, misleading drones to incorrect locations and compromising missions

Critical Risk

Mission Failure

Loss of accurate positioning leads to failed operations, increased risks, and compromised safety in critical missions
